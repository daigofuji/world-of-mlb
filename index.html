There were 1,533 total players that appeared in 2018 regular season (*There are duplicates, because traded players will show up multiple times) and they are born in 26 different countries and territories. Here is the breakdown of those players by country of birth according to baseball-reference: 1,100 are from United States, 158 from Dominican Republic, 117 Venezuela, 30 Cuba, 28 Puerto Rico, 23 Mexico, 12 Canada, 9 Japan, 8 South Korea, 7 Colombia, 6 Curacao, 6 Panama, 4 Brazil, 4 Germany, 4 Netherlands, 4 Taiwan, 3 Australia, 2 U. S. Virgin Islands, 1 Aruba, 1 United Kingdom, 1 Guam, 1 Hong Kong, 1 Lithuania, 1 Nicaragua, 1 Saudi Arabia, and 1 South Africa.
